Overview
RuleScript lets you write policy specifications that are simple, unambiguous, and are applied with utter consistency by a computer. It works by providing a framework for the following equation:
input documents + validation rules = pass/fail decision
You write your rules in a simple computer language, point the rules at input document(s), and receive an instant, automatic answer about whether the input conforms to your rules.
RuleScript is fast and flexible enough to save time on a one-week project. It's also built on industrial-strength software that can scale to hundreds of team members.
Features
Learn to write it in 1 hour. Learn to read it in 1 minute.
Computer languages don't have to be scary. RuleScript is easy to follow after a short primer on the ground rules. There's no math required, and no weird symbols to learn.
Get the correct result instantly.
Once a policy is defined using RuleScript, it can instantly assess whether an input document conforms to the policy. Have thousands of documents? No sweat.
Iterate quickly, whether prototyping or upgrading.
RuleScript takes the guesswork out of policy development. Make a change to your policy and instantly see its effects on your inputs.
